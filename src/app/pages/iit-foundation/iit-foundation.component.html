IIT Foundation

What is IIT JEE / NEET Foundation? IIT JEE / NEET foundation is a starting point (building block) for aspirants in class 8, 9 and 10. The foundation course helps aspirants become mentally and strategically strong when they start studying IIT JEE / NEET concepts and topics, ideally from class 11 onwards.
