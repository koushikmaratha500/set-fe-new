All you need to know about TS - EAMCET

What is TS EAMCET?

Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture & Medical Entrance Test (TS - EAMCET) is a state-level entrance exam, organised by Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad on behalf of the Council of Higher Education (TSCHE).
